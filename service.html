What We Do?

We believe in the power of culture.

Choir

Our choir brings together individuals with a passion for singing, performing both traditional Chinese songs and contemporary music.

Dance Troupe

Our dance troupe showcases a variety of traditional and modern Chinese dances, performing at various cultural events and festivals.

Rose Valley Single Club

A vibrant social club for singles, offering a space to meet new friends and participate in engaging activities.

Tours Group

We organize cultural tours and trips, providing opportunities to explore new places and experience different cultures.

Study Abroad and Immigration Services

We offer comprehensive support for those seeking educational opportunities abroad or assistance with immigration processes.

Social Events and Charitable Performances

Throughout the year, we host a range of social events and performances aimed at fostering community spirit and giving back to society.
